Output

  1. Raw: The signal is displayed as recieved
  2. Calculate: [darkline - signal ] / highlevel
  3. Transmittance: if [darkline - base] is lower than transmissionlevel the signal will be set to 100.
    The idea is that the spectrum of the light source is here not strong enought.
    At the other points y = 100*[(darkline-signal)/high_level]/[(darkline-base)/high_level]
  4. Absorbance: if [darkline - base] is lower than transmissionlevel the signal will be set to 0.
    At the other points y = log10[(darkline-base)/high_level]/[(darkline-signal)/high_level]
Here is room for improvement I think.
