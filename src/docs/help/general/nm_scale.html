nm scale

The idea behind scaling the x-axis in nanometer is taking two spectra with one defined maximun at a distinct frequency. These spectra are available by laserpointer.

Howto

  1. Take a spectrum of a e.g. 405nm laser with one peak and save it under a filename 405nm.csv.
    the first three numbers give the program the frequency
  2. Same procedure with a longer wavelength.
  3. Hit the [nm]-button
